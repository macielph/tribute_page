Daft Punk

12 Million Album Sales Worldwide.

Six Grammy Awards

Get Lucky

Get Lucky is currently, Daft Punk’s most popular single. In the United States, the song debuted at number five on the Billboard Dance/Electronic Songs chart. The song then reached the number two performance and eventually reached number one and remained at the top for 13 weeks. The song has sold more than 9.3 million copies, making it one of the best-selling songs of all time and winning two Grammy Awards.

Tron: Legacy

Tron: Legacy is the soundtrack album to the 2010 film Tron: Legacy. This is the first film score by Daft Punk. The film made $400 million during its worldwide theatrical run. The film was also nominated for an Oscar for Best Sound Editing.

Random Access Memories

Random Access Memories is Daft Punk’s fourth studio album featuring musicians like Paul Williams, Nile Rodgers, Giorgio Moroder, and Pharrel Williams. Random Access Memories is now certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America. The thirteen-track album went on to win 4 Grammy Awards.
